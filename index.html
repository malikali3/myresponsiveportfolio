Your Path Forward

Choose Your Future

Business and Accountancy are among the most future-proof Cambridge subjects. Explore where each pathway can take you.

🎯

Business Studies gives you a working model of how the world actually runs — how companies are built, funded, marketed and led. It's less about memorising theory and more about learning to think like a decision-maker.

  • Understand how companies really work
  • Sharpen decision-making under pressure
  • Build leadership instincts early
  • Learn marketing, finance basics & entrepreneurship
  • Practice real-world, transferable skills
🎓

A Business background opens the door to a wide spread of degrees, each leading somewhere different:

  • BBA / BS Business Administration — broad management foundation
  • Marketing — brands, campaigns, consumer behaviour
  • Human Resource Management — people, culture, talent
  • International Business — global trade & strategy
  • Supply Chain & Management — operations that keep companies running
  • Entrepreneurship — building ventures from scratch
  • Economics & Public Administration — policy, markets, governance
💼

Business graduates step into roles that exist in almost every company, at every stage:

  • Entrepreneur / Startup Founder
  • Business or Management Consultant
  • Marketing / Brand / Product Manager
  • HR, Operations & Supply Chain Manager
  • Project Manager / Business Analyst
  • CEO — the long game

Because business skills apply everywhere, the opportunities compound as you gain experience.

🤖

AI is very good at automating repetitive tasks. It is not good at the things business is really about:

  • Strategic decisions & business model creation
  • Leadership & negotiation
  • Innovation & opportunity recognition
  • Creativity, ethics & customer understanding

AI needs business leaders to turn technology into successful companies.

🌍

A Business background travels. Graduates work across:

  • Healthcare, Technology & Finance
  • Government & NGOs
  • Manufacturing & Retail
  • Consulting & International Organizations

Excellent global employability — wherever you go, business goes with you.

🧮

Accountancy is often called the language of business — every company, in every country, needs it. It trains a specific kind of mind:

  • Real financial literacy
  • Sharp problem solving & analytical thinking
  • Precision under pressure
  • Confident, evidence-based decision making

High demand, globally — accountants are needed everywhere, always.

🎓

An Accountancy background leads naturally into:

  • BS Accounting — core technical foundation
  • Accounting & Finance — blends both disciplines
  • Finance & Banking — capital markets & institutions
  • Commerce — broad business & trade grounding
  • Economics — how markets actually behave
  • Auditing — specialised assurance track
📜

Beyond a degree, these qualifications open doors worldwide:

ACCA

Globally recognised. Covers audit, tax, finance & consulting — respected inside multinational companies.

CA — Chartered Accountant

The classic route into public practice, audit firms & senior financial leadership.

ICAEW

International recognition, strong footing in corporate finance, advisory & leadership.

CFA

Built for investment — portfolio management, equity research, asset management & investment banking.

Taxation

Tax consultant, corporate & international tax, or a role inside government tax departments.

💼

Accountancy careers rarely stay narrow — they branch:

  • Chartered Accountant / Auditor / Internal Auditor
  • Financial Analyst / Investment Analyst
  • Finance Manager / Financial Controller / CFO
  • Tax Consultant / Budget Analyst / Risk Analyst
  • Banking Officer / Treasury Manager
🤖

AI can now handle a lot of routine bookkeeping. What it can't do is stand behind the numbers. Humans remain essential for:

  • Financial judgement & strategy
  • Auditing, ethics & regulation
  • Tax planning & corporate governance
  • Risk management, compliance & decision making

AI assists accountants — it does not replace trusted financial professionals.
